Code Bingo

Entry: Team of 2: 50/-
Prize Money: 200/-

Instructions: Each team will receive a 5*5 bingo card with numbers filled in it randomly and 25 coding questions to solve in the hackerrank platform. Players should mark the square of that particular question number they solved, on the bingo card given to them. Then, they need to cross the marked boxes to form a full length line (vertical or horizontal or diagonal) The team which solves the questions fastly and strikes all the letters in BINGO on the given card is the winner.
